Carpaccio

  • Time: 5-10 minutes
  • Serves:
Ingredients Quantity
Raw veal 300g
Raw fish 300g
Raw vension 300g
Lemon 1 slice

Step 1: Slightly freeze beef tenderloin, then slice it as thin as possible.

Step 2:Lay slices on a plate, cover with plastic, gently pound to paper‑thin.

Step 3:Drizzle with good olive oil and a little lemon juice, season with salt and pepper.

Sushi

  • Time: 30-60 minutes
  • Serves:
Ingredients Quantity
Raw fish 300g
Nori 1
Vinegared rice 300g

Step 1:Cook short‑grain rice, season warm rice with rice vinegar, sugar, salt; let cool.

Step 2:Slice very fresh sashimi‑grade fish into thin pieces.

Step 3:Form small rice ovals with damp hands, add a tiny bit of wasabi on top.

Step 4:Press fish slice onto rice gently, serve right away with soy sauce and pickled ginger.

Steak Tartare

  • Time: 20-30 minutes
  • Serves:
Ingredients Quantity
Raw ground meat 300g
Raw yolk 1

Step 1:Finely dice very cold beef tenderloin with a sharp knife.

Step 2:Finely chop shallot, capers, cornichons, parsley.

Step 3:Mix beef with veggies, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire, salt, pepper.

Step 4:Shape on a plate, top with a raw egg yolk, serve immediately with toasted bread.
